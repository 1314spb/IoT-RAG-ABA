- Domain: Independent and Self-help

- Task: Tooth Brushing

- Sub task: Squeezing toothpaste onto brush

- Description: Teaching the client to independently squeeze an appropriate amount of toothpaste onto a toothbrush as part of the overall tooth brushing routine.

- Materials:
  * Toothbrush
  * Toothpaste
  * Visual schedule or task analysis chart
  * Timer (optional)

- Procedure:
  1. Place materials within reach of the client
  2. Present the visual schedule or task analysis chart
  3. Provide verbal instruction: "Let's brush teeth. First, put toothpaste on the brush."
  4. Use least-to-most prompting hierarchy:
     a. Verbal prompt
     b. Gestural prompt
     c. Partial physical prompt
     d. Full physical prompt
  5. Gradually fade prompts as client demonstrates increased independence
  6. Provide immediate reinforcement for successful completion

- Data Collection:
  * Record level of prompting required for each trial
  * Track successful independent completions
  * Note duration of task completion (if using timer)

- Variations:
  * Practice with different types of toothpaste containers (e.g., tube, pump)
  * Incorporate into full tooth brushing routine
  * Practice at different times of day

- Reinforcement:
  * Verbal praise for attempts and successful completion
  * Token system with preferred reward for consistent performance
  * Natural reinforcement of fresh, clean mouth feeling

- Skills Developed:
  * Fine motor skills
  * Following multi-step instructions
  * Task initiation and completion
  * Personal hygiene
  * Independence in daily living skills
