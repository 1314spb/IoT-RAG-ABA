- Domain: Communication
- Task: Teaching Eye Contact during Conversations
- Sub task: Initiating and maintaining eye contact with a therapist during a conversation
- Description: This task targets the development of eye contact skills, which is an essential aspect of effective communication. The therapist will engage in a conversation with the individual and encourage them to maintain eye contact.
- Materials: 
  - A conversation script
  - A timer
  - A visual aid (e.g., a picture or a video) to support the conversation
- Procedure: 
  1. Begin a conversation with the individual using the conversation script.
  2. Initial the conversation with a question or a statement that encourages the individual to make eye contact.
  3. When the individual makes eye contact, provide reinforcement (e.g., praise, a sticker, or a small reward).
  4. Gradually increase the duration of the conversation and the frequency of the reinforcement.
  5. If the individual breaks eye contact, calmly and gently prompt them to maintain eye contact.
- Data Collection: 
  - Record the number of times the individual makes eye contact during the conversation.
  - Record the duration of the individual's eye contact during the conversation.
  - Note any challenges or difficulties the individual experiences during the task.
- Variations: 
  - Use different conversation topics or scripts to increase the variety of the task.
  - Increase the duration of the conversation or the number of conversations.
  - Use different visual aids or props to support the conversation.
  - Incorporate gestures or other nonverbal cues to encourage eye contact.
- Reinforcement: 
  - Verbal praise (e.g., "Great job maintaining eye contact!")
  - Nonverbal praise (e.g., a thumbs-up or a smile)
  - Tangible rewards (e.g., stickers, small toys, or treats)
  - Social reinforcement (e.g., "I like the way you're looking at me!")
- Skills Developed: 
  - Initiating and maintaining eye contact during conversations
  - Developing effective communication skills
  - Improving social interaction and relationships
  - Enhancing self-esteem and confidence
