- Domain: Independent and Self-help
- Task: Meal Preparation and Cooking
- Sub task: Preparing a Simple Meal
- Description: Teach the client to independently prepare a simple meal, such as a sandwich or a salad, with minimal support from the therapist or caregiver.
- Materials: Ingredients for the meal, cooking utensils and appliances, recipe cards or pictures
- Procedure: 
  1. Begin by teaching the client to read and follow a recipe, using pictures and numbers.
  2. Practice measuring ingredients and combining them according to the recipe.
  3. Teach the client to use basic cooking utensils and appliances, such as a toaster or a microwave.
  4. Gradually increase the complexity of the meal by introducing new ingredients or cooking techniques.
  5. Use visual supports such as pictures or a recipe app on a tablet to aid the client.
- Data Collection: 
  1. Track the number of meals the client is able to prepare independently.
  2. Measure the time it takes for the client to complete the meal preparation task.
  3. Assess the client's ability to follow the recipe and use cooking utensils and appliances correctly.
- Variations: 
  1. Practice preparing more complex meals, such as a casserole or a stir-fry.
  2. Introduce new cooking techniques, such as grilling or roasting.
  3. Increase the amount of time the client has to complete the meal preparation task.
- Reinforcement: 
  1. Praise the client for their efforts and accomplishments.
  2. Use a token economy system to reward the client for completing the meal preparation task.
  3. Provide a choice of healthy snacks or meals as a reward for successful completion of the task.
- Skills Developed: 
  1. Independent living skills
  2. Problem-solving skills
  3. Cooking skills
  4. Time management skills
  5. Self-reliance
