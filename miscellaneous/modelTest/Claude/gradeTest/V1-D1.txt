- Domain: Academic and Learning
- Task: Category: Expressive
- Subtask: Naming Shapes
- Description: The learner will correctly name basic geometric shapes when presented with visual representations.
- Materials: Flashcards or physical objects representing various shapes (circle, square, triangle, rectangle, oval)
- Procedure:
  1. Present a shape to the learner
  2. Ask, "What shape is this?"
  3. Wait up to 5 seconds for a response
  4. Provide praise for correct answers or gentle correction for incorrect answers
  5. Repeat with different shapes
- Data Collection: Record correct/incorrect responses for each trial, calculate percentage of correct responses per session
- Variations:
  1. Use real-world objects that represent shapes
  2. Ask learner to draw the shape when given its name
  3. Present multiple shapes and ask learner to identify a specific one
- Reinforcement: Verbal praise, stickers, or small rewards for correct responses or improvements
- Skills Developed: Expressive language, shape recognition, vocabulary expansion, visual discrimination
